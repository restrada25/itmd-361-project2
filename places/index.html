Adventure Time!

Indiana Dunes

Indiana Dunes is a beautiful place. It has a park within the forest and places to have cookouts. Some of the trails can be really hard since the sand makes it harder to walk. However, getting to the beach after finishing the trail makes it worth it.

Starved Rock

Starved Rock is a must visit if you are in Illinois. It is bigger than Indiana Dunes and it has many beautiful areas.
